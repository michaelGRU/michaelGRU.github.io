SQL Database for Family Farms

The problem we try to solve:

In the world of data-centric decisions, there tends to be information asymmetry between big corporations and small businesses whereby the former possess a competitive advantage over the latter with better information and more resources. With the increasing risks of COVID-19 and this imbalance in power favoring big corporations, small local businesses are facing many problems regarding their supply chain, consumer behavior, and waste reduction systems. The goal for this project is to create a user-friendly database with an interactive interface to analyze data from different business functions to improve the production system of a farm, boost its sales, and reduce waste. For instance, by tracking and analyzing customer data, farm owners can gain insights into the optimal balance between supply and demand. Furthermore, a database that tracks employee information can provide useful information about personnel efficiency. The database can identify what is lacking in certain divisions and what areas are self sustained without additional investments. Lastly, we can also improve product quality by monitoring the health and activity of the farm’s livestock. You can find the source code here: SQL_farm
